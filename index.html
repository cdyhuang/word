Lead Word

Guess the missing word from headlines and lead sentences.

How to play

Pick a difficulty and optional section (Business, Politics, etc.). You’ll see a headline or lead with one word blanked out. Type the word and guess. Wrong? You get another headline or lead with the same word missing—up to 5 (or 3 on Hard). After each guess you’ll see the story it came from. Minor typos count as correct.

Choose difficulty

Same word is missing in every sentence. You get up to 5 sentences to guess it.

Focus on a section?

  • Easy: Common words, clear context
  • Medium: Familiar topics, varied vocabulary
  • Hard: Less common words, only 3 clues (no second chances)
